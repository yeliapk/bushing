Read sentences aloud

Read the sentences according to the prompts. You can click the corresponding button to play the sentence, and click the button many times to achieve the effect of repeated practice

Film dubbing mode

Users can also enter the Movie Dubbing mode. Users can choose appropriate movie clips according to their own needs and characteristics.
